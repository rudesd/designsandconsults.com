Local Search Optimization

GET FOUND
locally.

When a homeowner searches for plumbers or roofers nearby, does your business show up? RJM's local SEO service is built to get contractors to the top of Google in their service area — and keep them there.

What's Included

What You Get

Local SEO for contractors is different from generic SEO. It's about dominating Google Maps, owning your city's search results, and building the kind of online presence that makes customers pick up the phone.

  • Google Business Profile full optimization and management
  • Local citation building across 50+ directories (Yelp, Angi, HomeAdvisor, etc.)
  • On-page SEO for your website targeting local search keywords
  • Review generation strategy to build your 5-star reputation
  • Monthly ranking reports showing exactly where you stand

How It Works

The Process

01
